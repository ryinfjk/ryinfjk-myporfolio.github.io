What makes me want to be a surgeon? 

People have many ambitions in life. Some want to be famous, some want to be inventors. My life goals are a bit different. My goal in life is to become a cardiothoracic surgeon. Becoming a cardiothoracic surgeon is a goal I want to achieve not only to help those close to me but also to help those who have loved ones with heart problems.

Of all the surgeries, only cardiothoracic surgery is the only specialty where the surgeon can stop the function of the organ at his wish and start it at his will. This makes me thrill.

Lastly It's because cardio thoracic surgery is truly awesome. Everything is life or death. That is pretty baller.
